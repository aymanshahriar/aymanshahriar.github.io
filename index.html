Welcome to The UCalgary Ramen Lovers Club!

We are a small but passionate group of people who love all things ramen. The university of Calgary's Ramen Lovers Club was founded in 2013 and since then it has grown to be one of the most active groups in the university. We hold all sorts of ramen appreciation events year rounds, as well as various cooking competetions and member meet and greets. Please go to our "Ramen Types" page to view some of the various ramen that exist. Joining the Ramen Lovers Club will be a fullfilling experience that will be one of the highlights of your university life!
